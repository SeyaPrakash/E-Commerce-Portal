About company

Located in India, Tamil Nadu. Guitars has been helping musicians with their needs since 2018. We started as a guitar accessory shop. We've since grown into a full-blown retail store providing great deals on brand name gear. We also offer guitar and amp repairs, setups, upgrades, and string replacements at reasonable costs. Every customer is essential to us, and we strive for you to have a quality, friendly experience whether you're shopping online or in-store. With many years of practice and performance background, we share our trade secrets with you! Whether it be lessons or advice on music gear, let us help guide you on your musical journey!
